Thick Chocolate Shake

Description

It is a rich chocolate treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • ¼ cup powdered chocolate drink mix
  • 1 teaspoon powdered egg whites

Steps

  1. Add the ice cream in blender
  2. Add milk
  3. Add chocolate drink mix
  4. Add powdered egg whites
  5. Blend until smooth. You may need to stop and stir once or twice to get it evenly mixed
